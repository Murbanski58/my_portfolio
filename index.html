Senior Capstone Project: Building a Predictive Model to Forecast Voluntary Turnover

Through the duration of my final semester in the Tippie College of Business at The University of Iowa, I completed a group project in which I led my team and myslef, in collaboration with a manufacturing company, on a groundbreaking initiative. We partnered to build a predictive model aimed at forecasting voluntary employee turnover, and delved deep into analyzing the key factors influencing these decisions. Utilizing cutting-edge tools such as R, rattle, and Tableau, we harnessed the power of data to uncover patterns and insights that can shape strategic decision-making within the organization. My team and I initiated the predictive modeling process to develop a model capable of anticipating voluntary employee departures. Leveraging the capabilities of R, we meticulously cleansed the provided data, eliminating extraneous variables and those with high correlation to our target variable. Creating both an AdaBoost model Administrative a Logisitc Regression model, we successfully identified key variables leading employees to leave a company with an accuracy of 77%.
